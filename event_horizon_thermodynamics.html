I. Theoretical Framework

We model the black hole as a thermodynamic system radiating as a blackbody. The primary challenge is the non-linear relationship between mass and temperature, leading to a "runaway" effect as the mass approaches the Planck limit.

\[ \frac{dM}{dt} = -\frac{\alpha \hbar c^4}{G^2 M^2} \]
The constant \(\alpha\) accounts for the number of particle species emitted. This simulation assumes a pure Schwarzschild vacuum where \(\alpha \approx 1/15360\pi\).
